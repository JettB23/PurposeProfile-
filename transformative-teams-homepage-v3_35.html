About

Meet Piet Pistorius.

Piet is a leadership trainer, speaker, entrepreneur, and the creator of the Purpose Profile framework. For more than two decades he's mentored leaders, coached students, and walked alongside people in the messy middle of transformation — across cultures, businesses, and ministry environments.

His passion is simple: helping people return to their original design, so they can live and lead with clarity and confidence.

— Clarity changes everything.

The book

Start living by design.

The Purpose Profile isn't another personality test — it's about remembering what's already wired into you. Drawing on the seven core gifts, what Piet calls your identity architecture, the book includes 21 combination chapters exploring how your gifts layer to form the unique design that is you.

For anyone tired of drifting: stop asking "Who am I?" and start asking "How was I designed?"
